About us

Founded in 2020

At Ableabe AB, we believe in creating products that inspire movement, fun, and connection. Our flagship brand, FeeBee, introduces a new twist to outdoor play with our innovative mini frisbee. Designed for both casual fun and serious play, FeeBee is more than just a toy—it's a way to bring people together, challenge skills, and enjoy the outdoors.

We specialize in social media-driven sales, leveraging the power of digital platforms to connect with our customers where they are most active. Our commitment to quality, creativity, and community drives us to continuously develop products that not only entertain but also encourage active lifestyles.

At Ableabe AB, we're more than a company—we're a community of enthusiasts dedicated to making every throw, every catch, and every moment count. Join us as we redefine outdoor play and bring a little more joy to the world, one frisbee at a time.
